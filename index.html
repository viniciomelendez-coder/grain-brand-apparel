🖨️ DTF Transfer Costs
▼
ConfirmedEditable

Source: Ninja Transfers — May 2025 (prices locked through end of 2025)

DTF priced at $0.02/sq inch on gang sheets. A full-size shirt front (12"×16" = 192 sq in) = $3.84 at max bulk. Small orders (1–5 pcs) run higher because gang sheets aren't full — practical cost $4.50–$5.50/pc. At 100+ pcs: $2.50–$3.50/pc with cumulative discounts up to 50% off.

Note: Ninja's cumulative discount counts all transfers in your order. 250 total pieces = 50% off all transfers.

🪡 Embroidered Patch Costs
▼
ConfirmedEditable

Source: Ninja Patches — May 2025

Embroidered patches start at $1.42/pc at minimum order of 25 pcs. Bulk discounts up to 74% off on large quantities. A 4" max patch on apparel — heat pressed on with heat seal backing.

At 25 pcs: ~$1.42 | At 100 pcs: ~$0.90 | At 300+ pcs: ~$0.60 estimated based on discount structure.

⬛ PVC Rubber Patch Costs
▼
ConfirmedEditable

Source: Ninja Patches — May 2025

PVC Rubber patches start at $2.23/pc at 25 pcs minimum. More premium than embroidered — 3D look, Pantone color precision, waterproof. Max size for apparel: 4".

At 25 pcs: ~$2.23 | At 100 pcs: ~$1.50 | At 300+ pcs: ~$1.00 estimated. Bulk discounts up to 70% off.

📊 Market Pricing Comparison
▼
Market Research

What the market charges for custom apparel DTF 2025:
Single tee: $25–$45 retail
Small run (6–20): $18–$32/pc
Mid bulk (21–50): $15–$28/pc
Wholesale (100+): $12–$22/pc

Hoodies add $10–$20 to all of the above.

Your Medium margin lands 10–20% below average market — competitive and profitable for a growing small business. Use Soft for new clients or large volume, Aggressive for rush or loyal clients.
